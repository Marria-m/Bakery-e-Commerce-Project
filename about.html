Our Story

Sips & Bites began as a dream born from my love for bakeries and the magical feeling of being surrounded by fresh crusts and hot coffee.

I started this mini e-commerce website because I genuinely love bakeries and the feeling of being there. The fresh crust and hot coffee create a whole world for me, and I'm sure for many other people too. I wanted to create something that others would really love to have in their lives - to make their houses feel like a small bakery or cozy café when they order from this website.

I tried to make it as perfect as I could with the information and knowledge I have about frontend development. I'm happy with the output and I hope I can do even better next time. Thanks to anyone who sees this or wants to support this project!
